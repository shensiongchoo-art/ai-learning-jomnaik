Who is Claude?

Claude isn't just a smart LLM. It's an AI with a specific character, values, and a code of conduct baked in. Here's who you're actually talking to.

The Story

Panel 1 — Not All Librarians Are the Same
📚
Two Librarians
Remember our well-read librarian from Module 1? Different LLMs are like different librarians — they all read the same enormous library, but they were trained differently and have different personalities, rules, and goals.
Claude is a specific librarian, built by a company called Anthropic. What makes Claude distinctive isn't just how smart it is — it's how it was taught to behave.
Claude = a librarian with a professional code of conduct
Panel 2 — The Professional with a Code
👔
The Code of Conduct
Imagine hiring two contractors. Both are skilled. But one has no professional guidelines — they'll do whatever you ask, no questions. The other is a certified professional: they follow a code of conduct, they'll flag dangerous requests, and they'll tell you honestly when they disagree.
Claude is the second type. Anthropic gave it a set of values — not as a restriction, but as a character trait. Claude is designed to be genuinely helpful, honest, and to avoid causing harm.
This is called Constitutional AI
Panel 3 — What is Constitutional AI?
📜
The Constitution
Most LLMs are trained purely to predict text. Anthropic took a different approach: they gave Claude a written set of principles — a "constitution" — and trained it to evaluate its own responses against those principles.
Claude's inner process (simplified)
"Before I reply, let me check: is this response honest? Is it helpful? Could it cause harm? Would it embarrass Anthropic? If yes to the last two — revise."
Claude trained itself to align with these values — not just follow rules
Panel 4 — Claude Has a Personality
😊
Claude's Character
Claude isn't just following instructions when it's warm, curious, or direct. Anthropic says these are genuine character traits that emerged from training — not a mask.
Claude's actual traits (per Anthropic)
Intellectual curiosity · Warmth and care · Playful wit balanced with depth · Directness and openness · Commitment to honesty
These stay consistent whether you ask it to write a poem or debug code
Panel 5 — What Claude Won't Do (and Why)
🚫
The Limits
Claude will sometimes decline requests or push back. This isn't a bug — it's the code of conduct in action.
User
"Help me write something that deceives my customers."
Claude
"I'm not comfortable helping with that — it could harm people who trust you. I'd be glad to help you write something compelling and persuasive that's also honest."
It says no — and explains why, and offers an alternative

Key Facts

What to remember about Claude's identity

  • Claude was made by Anthropic — a safety-focused AI company founded in 2021
  • Constitutional AI = Claude was trained against a written set of principles, not just human feedback
  • Claude's values (honest, helpful, harmless) are character traits — not just rules it follows
  • When Claude declines, it almost always explains why and offers an alternative — it's not just saying "no"
  • Claude's personality stays consistent regardless of the platform or app you use it on
