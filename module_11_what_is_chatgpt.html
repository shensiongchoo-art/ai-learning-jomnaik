Phase 3 · ChatGPT & Codex

What is ChatGPT? 📰

Meet the world-famous AI assistant — and understand exactly how it works through the story of a very well-read columnist.

🎯 The Big Analogy

Imagine a Smart Newspaper Columnist who spent years reading every book, article, website, and conversation ever written. She didn't memorise it all word-for-word — she absorbed the patterns, ideas, and style of human writing.

Now she sits at her desk. You give her a topic. She writes. Fast. Fluently. On almost anything. That columnist is ChatGPT.

📚
Training Data
Scene 1 — The Education

She Read Everything

Before ChatGPT could write a single word, it "read" a huge chunk of the internet — books, Wikipedia, code, news, conversations — billions of examples of human language.

I didn't memorise the words. I learned the patterns.
Scene 2 — Your Request

You Hand Her a Prompt

You type a message. ChatGPT sees that as the start of a story she needs to continue — word by word, predicting what comes next based on everything she learned.

Tell me what you need. I'll find the right words.

The Output
Scene 3 — The Output

She Writes — Word by Word

ChatGPT generates text one word (token) at a time. Each word it picks influences the next. It's not copying from a database — it's generating fresh text, every time.

I wrote this fresh for you. No copy-paste from anywhere.
Scene 4 — The Interface

ChatGPT = The Product

ChatGPT is the app — the chat interface you talk to at chat.openai.com. Underneath it runs a model called GPT (like GPT-4). Think of ChatGPT as the shop, GPT as the engine inside.

I'm the columnist you talk to. GPT is my brain.

📅 ChatGPT Through the Ages

📖
2022 · Launch

ChatGPT (GPT-3.5)

Launched November 2022. Shocked the world — 1 million users in 5 days. Good at conversation, but sometimes made things up confidently.

🧠
2023 · GPT-4

ChatGPT Plus with GPT-4

Smarter, more reliable, can understand images. A big leap in reasoning. Introduced "plugins" — letting ChatGPT browse the web or use tools.

⚡
2024 · GPT-4o

GPT-4o ("Omni")

Much faster. Handles text, images, and voice in one model. "Omni" means it can do many things. Voice felt more natural than ever.

🚀
2025–2026 · Now

GPT-4.5 / o1 / o3 / o4 Family

OpenAI released "reasoning" models (o1, o3, o4) that think step-by-step before answering — better at math, logic, and complex problems. The columnist now drafts and edits before she writes.

🔍 ChatGPT vs. Search Engine — What's the Difference?

🔎 Google Search

📋

Finds pages — lists websites that already have the answer

🔗

Gives you links to go read elsewhere

🕐

Always current — indexes new pages daily

✅

Source is traceable — you see where the info comes from

💬 ChatGPT

✍️

Generates answers — writes a fresh response for you

🗣️

Converses — remembers what you said earlier in the chat

📅

Knowledge cut-off — may not know recent events (unless web search is on)

⚠️

Can make things up — sounds confident even when wrong (hallucination!)
